Flooring type
?

  • vitrified Tiles

    Vitrified tile is a ceramic tile with very low porosity and are often used outdoors due to their water and frost resistance.

  • Ceramic Porcelain Tiles

    Made from heated, refined clay, porcelain tile offers an increasingly popular alternative to natural stone and ceramic tiles.

  • Anti Skid Tiles

    These tiles are rough in surface and are mainly used for area where there is a danger of slipping.

  • Marble Granite Tiles

    These tiles are ideal for decorating in a rustic-themed room, and are resistant to stains and moisture.

  • Wooden flooring

    Wooden flooring as the name suggests is any flooring manufactured from timber/wood that is designed for structural or aesthetic use.

  • RAK/laminated wooden flooring

    RAK/laminated wooden flooring (also known as floating wood flooring) is created using lamination process and is used to simulate wooden surfaces.

  • Wood Finish Vinyl Flooring

    These are highly durable, low maintenance and low-cost flooring which give the user the grandeur of wooden finish.
